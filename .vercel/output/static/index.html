Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our IPTV service.

What is IPTV and how does it work?
IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers television content over Internet Protocol networks. Unlike traditional TV services, IPTV allows you to stream content on-demand through your internet connection, offering more flexibility and content options.

What devices can I use to watch?
Our IPTV service works on multiple devices including Smart TVs, Android boxes, smartphones, tablets, and computers. We support popular apps like Smart IPTV, Perfect Player, and more.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 24-hour money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service. Simply contact our support team within 24 hours of purchase.

How fast should my internet connection be?
We recommend a minimum internet speed of 10 Mbps for HD streaming. For 4K content, a speed of 25 Mbps or higher is recommended for the best experience.
